Rapid Pandemic Medical Response Management

Don't be a New York!

The idea is to make an app to help local officials effectively manage the medical resources in their area. This helps with keeping hospitals/camps stay in sync with the local officials. This lack of synchronization can be a serious problem. This application will increase bureaucratic efficiency and provide a centralized interface to the officials for a more effective response in both gathering information and taking action.
